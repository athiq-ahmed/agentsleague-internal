Certification exams are challenging — and many self-study candidates struggle to know when they’re truly ready to book.

AI Powered Certification Preparation app solves this with a production-grade multi-agent system built on Azure AI Foundry.

Now, lets show 3 main scenarios, and lets begin with as a new user and as you see the Azure AI services used in this application
 
in this case, lets take an example of Alex who is  a data scientist with 5 years of experience and looking for DP 100 certification , has AZ fundamentals certification, 8 hours a week, 6 weeks available.

Once you click on Create my AI study plan, 
the Guardrails will run and validate the details before any agent runs. 

As you can see we have multiple tabs, lets go one by one 

Tab one — Domain map  

It shows Alex is strong in 2 modules and other 2 modules needs focus. 

It also shows the weightage and the confidence against each module.

Now, lets go to the next tab that is 
Study setup page.

It shows if there are any pre-requisites as per the exam selected


It shows weekly study plan which requires a review or skip.

It also shows study hours by domain and a quick summary at the end.

Now lets move to the next tab in the application and that is Learning path 

It shows the exam specific modules with time estimates and difficulty tags.
